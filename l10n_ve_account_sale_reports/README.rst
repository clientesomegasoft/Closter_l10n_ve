Account and Sale Statistics
---------------------------

Customize the standards reports on Account and Sale applications

Express the monetary fields/columns that appears in some analysis
reports as amounts in the company's operative currency, useful for
business that work with more than a single currency in Venezuela.

The reports that are affected by this module are:

-  Invoices Statistics
-  Sales Analysis Report
